WE WHAT WE DO

We are thirilled to share our passion for books with you, valued readers

Being located in the heart of the city of Colombo we have been able to connect to thousands of Booklovers around the region while quenching their thirst for reading and fulfilling their passion for learning.

Over the past few years we have been the pioneer in bringing to the homes of the readers the best of books the world's got to offer. Let it be of any genre we got it all stocked just for your pleasure and entertainment!

Apart from our fancy website that draws thousands of new book lovers to us each day our store has been one of the best in the city. Designed with a future concept while preserving the standards of a state of the art bookstore.

Since the day of our beginning we have moved forward through many obstacles to offer our customers a great reading experience and exposture to the most iconic literature scripts ever written in history.

While connecting to our customers in person we have also adapted to the modern world to connect with our distant customers. No matter where you are in the world Chapters Bookstore will always be with you for your Book Needs.

We believe that our customers are the No. 1 reason for our success. Thats why we thrive to offer you the best. We promise to serve you better in the future while making sure that you have a memorable experience with us.
